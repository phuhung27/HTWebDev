Total Servings: 4 people


Gochujang Pasta

An amazing fusion dish that will leave you craving more.


Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Garlic & onions
  • Gochujang paste
  • Heavy cream
  • Brown sugar
  • Green onions
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Steak

Instructions

  1. Add a bit of salt to a pot of water and start to boil it. Add your pasta and cook until al-dente.
  2. While the pasta is boiling, add butter to your pan. Once the butter has melted, add garlic and onions.
  3. Cut up your steak into slices and fry it for around 5 mintues.
  4. Add some pasta water. After, you will add a spoon of gochujang paste and heavy cream. Stir nicely.
  5. Once your pasta is done, you can drain the water. Add the pasta into the pan once the steak is cooked nicely.
  6. Grade some parmesan cheese on top and stir the pasta around so it is fully absorbed in the sauce.
    Now enjoy!
